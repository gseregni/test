Easy enough to let stakeholders come in 

"Clients always change their mind". That's normal. Visualizing a final product from a mockup can be extremely difficult for average person. Pingendo instead enables clients to play with living pages since firsts scratch.

Works in a browser, offering a simplified Github interface to make project browsing and proposal of changes as pull-request deadly simple for everyone.

A truly agile alternative to mockups and CMS where customers see what will be delivered only when the site is almost finished. By that time, changes can be difficult, slow and costly to implement.

Every time you save, a new webpage version is stored on Github. It's reversible at any time, so no need to worry about messing-up.
